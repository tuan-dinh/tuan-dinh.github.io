I am a Ph.D. student in computer science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, with minor in statistics.
I'm currently working with Prof. Kangwook Lee. Previously, I have worked with Prof. Vikas Singh in learning generative models for graph-structured data and medical images. Prior to graduate studies, I have worked with Prof. Tru Cao in learning AI systems for disease forecast and healthcare.

Research

I'm currently interested in two questions: (1) how to make the adaptation of large pretrained models efficient and effective, (2) how to use machine learning to help address medical problems. Research will be more meaningful if it helps society.
