I am a postdoc at UCSF, working with Prof. Vasilis Ntranos and MAZE on Language Modeling of Proteins. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science (minor in Statistics) with Prof. Kangwook Lee, developing modular neural networks using pre-trained models. I have also spent my earlier years with Prof. Vikas Singh to study generative models for graph-structured data and with Prof. Tru Cao in building AI systems for disease forecast.

Research

My research interest is Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning, with my current focus being on large language models and modular deep learning. I am also working towards building and adapting language models for computational biology.
